Spokane Shelter Dashboard — Embed Snippet

Copy and paste the snippet below into any webpage to embed the live shelter map.

<iframe
  src="https://YOUR-DOMAIN/embed"
  width="100%"
  height="600"
  style="border:none;border-radius:12px;"
  title="Spokane Shelter Availability"
></iframe>

Replace YOUR-DOMAIN with your deployed domain (e.g. shelter-dashboard.vercel.app).

Increase height for taller displays. The map is fully responsive.
